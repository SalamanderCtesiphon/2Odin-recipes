Ceviche Recipe

Ceviche is a popular dish from Spain. It consist of either raw fish or shrimp which is "cooked" in lime juice. I know what your thinking. My Papaw would have called it fish bait. But if you think that this is fish bait, you would be very mistaken. It reminds me of pico de gallo with added substance.

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish or shrimp
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Clean and wash your vegetables
  2. Slice and dice your ingredients
  3. Place in a bowl
  4. Add lime juice
