Ceviche Recipe

Ceviche is a dish that is made with raw fish that is marinated in citrus juices. It is a popular dish in Latin America and is often served as an appetizer or a light meal. It is a very healthy dish that is low in calories and high in protein. It is also very easy to make and can be made with a variety of different fish. It is a great dish to make for a party or a family gathering.

Ingredients

  • Fresh fish or shrimp
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Lime

Steps

  1. Clean and wash your vegetables
  2. Slice and dice your ingredients
  3. Place in a bowl
  4. Add lime juice
