Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is a staple dish for members of the mafia. The dumb greasy fat Guido's love the stuff. The ancient Romans loved to eat putrid rotting fish. Luckily, today Italian cuisine is not as terrible as that of their ancestors. I love lasagna. Also Garfield the Cat loved lasagna, I hear. And as we all know hearsay is the only evidence we need. Am I right?

Ingredients

  • Microwave Oven
  • Plastic Forks
  • Paper Plates
  • Frozen Lasagna

Steps

  1. Remove frozen lasagna from box
  2. Poke a hole in the plastic film covering the lasagna
  3. Place in microwave
  4. Set timer on microwave
  5. Once cooking time is complete, allow lasagna 10 minutes to cool
  6. Serve with your favorite brand of malt liquor
