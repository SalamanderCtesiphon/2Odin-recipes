Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is the best thing ever. It's like a big, delicious, meaty, cheesy, --> pasta party in your mouth. It's the best. I love it. I love it so much. --> Whenever I am feeling hungry I know that there is only one dish that can satisfy my appetite and that is lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 Tbsp. dried oregano
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 24 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 lb. lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Boil lasagna noodles for 8 minutes.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, oregano, salt, and pepper over medium heat.
  4. Continue until the meat is browned and the onion is tender.
  5. Drain off the grease.
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread 1/2 cup of the marinara sauce.
  7. Arrange 6 lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread with half of the ricotta mixture, half of the remaining sauce, and half of the mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat the layers.
  10. Top with the remaining 6 noodles and sauce. Sprinkle with the parmesan cheese.
  11. Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly.
