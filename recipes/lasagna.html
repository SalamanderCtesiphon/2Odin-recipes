Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is the best thing ever. It's like a big, delicious, meaty, cheesy, --> pasta party in your mouth. It's the best. I love it. I love it so much. -->

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 Tbsp. dried oregano
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 24 oz. marinara sauce
  • 1 lb. lasagna noodles
  • 16 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, onion, oregano, salt, and pepper over medium heat until the meat is browned and the onion is tender. Drain off the grease.
  3. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread 1/2 cup of the marinara sauce. Arrange 6 lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread with half of the ricotta mixture, half of the remaining sauce, and half of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers. Top with the remaining 6 noodles and sauce. Sprinkle with the parmesan cheese.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly.
